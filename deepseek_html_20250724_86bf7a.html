About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Strategize, upgrade, and survive!

The Ultimate Survival Challenge

In Monster Survivors, you find yourself in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by terrifying creatures. Your mission is simple: survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters.

Each wave brings new challenges and more powerful enemies. You'll need to strategically choose upgrades, manage resources, and master combat mechanics to extend your survival time and climb the leaderboards.
